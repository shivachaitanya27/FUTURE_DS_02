

## 📊 Customer Churn & Retention Analysis (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer data from a telecom company to identify **churn patterns**, understand **key retention drivers**, and evaluate **customer lifetime value (CLV)**.

The goal is to provide actionable insights that help businesses **reduce customer loss and improve retention strategies**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify customers who are likely to churn
* Analyze factors influencing customer retention
* Segment customers based on behavior and value
* Estimate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Provide data-driven recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

* Handled missing values (especially in TotalCharges)
* Converted data types for accurate analysis
* Transformed categorical variables (Yes/No → 1/0)
* Created derived columns:

  * Churn Flag
  * Tenure Group
  * Charges Group

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Page 1: Churn Overview & Drivers

* Total Customers, Churn Customers, Churn Rate
* Churn by Contract Type
* Churn by Tenure Group
* Churn by Payment Method
* Churn by Tech Support
* Interactive slicers (Gender, Contract, Internet Service)

---

### 🔹 Page 2: Customer Insights & Value

* Customer Segmentation (Charges Group)
* Tenure Distribution
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Risk Segmentation (High / Medium / Low Risk)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn
* **New customers (0–1 year tenure)** are more likely to leave
* Customers without **Tech Support** show higher churn rates
* High-value customers tend to stay longer

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Improve onboarding experience for new customers
* Encourage long-term subscriptions with incentives
* Provide proactive support to high-risk customers
* Focus on retaining high-value customers
